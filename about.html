Centesimus Brokerage Trading Ltd (CBT) is a pioneering commodity trading member of the Commodity Market Exchange (CMX) duly licensed and regulated by the Capital Market Authority (CMA).We specialize in providing regulated access to structured trading of multicommodities offering comprehensive market information and facilitating domestic and international trade in various sectors.Our core services are trade finance and trade support for sector regulators and value chain actors,including farmers/producers,aggregators,traders,consumers and processors.

We operate as a trusted intermediary within the commodity trading ecosystem,bridging the gap between producers,consumers and traders.With a solid foundation in regulatory compliance and market expertise,we facilitate transparent,efficient and secure trading activities accross diverse commodity market.
